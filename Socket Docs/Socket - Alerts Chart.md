### Table Chart of Socket Alerts Operationalization

| **Alert Type**                           | **Action**                                                                                             | **Follow-up**                                                                                          | **Priority**        |
|------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| **Known Malware**                        | Block the package immediately.                                                                         | Notify the team and stakeholders. Investigate usage in the codebase and remove or replace the package.  | High                |
| **AI Detected Potential Malware**        | Treat with high suspicion. Block the package temporarily.                                              | Conduct a manual code review. Discuss with the security team to confirm and decide on further actions.  | High                |
| **Critical CVE**                         | Assess the impact on the system. Patch or update the package immediately.                               | Perform a security audit to ensure no breaches have occurred. Communicate updates to the team.          | High                |
| **Potential Typo Squat**                 | Verify the package name and compare it with the popular packages it mimics.                             | Remove and replace the package if confirmed as a typo squat. Notify the team to prevent future occurrences. | High                |
| **Protestware or Unwanted Behavior**     | Examine the package functionality.                                                                     | Replace the package if the behavior is confirmed. Inform the team and update documentation.             | Medium              |
| **Unstable Ownership**                   | Monitor the package for any unusual updates.                                                           | Consider finding alternative packages. Notify the team of potential risks.                              | Medium              |
| **New Author**                           | Keep an eye on updates from the new author.                                                            | Perform periodic reviews of the package. Notify the team of the new author for awareness.               | Medium              |
| **AI Detected Security Risk**            | Perform a thorough security review of the package.                                                     | Fix vulnerabilities if found or replace the package. Alert the team and provide details of the findings and actions taken. | Medium              |
| **HTTP/Git Dependency**                  | Review the dependency to ensure its integrity.                                                         | Consider fetching dependencies from more reliable sources or pinning specific versions. Update the team on the changes. | Medium              |
| **Non-existent Author**                  | Investigate the reason for the non-existent author.                                                    | If the package is otherwise secure, monitor its usage. Document the investigation results.              | Low                 |
| **Unpopular Package**                    | Assess the necessity of the package.                                                                   | Consider replacing it with a more popular and well-maintained package.                                  | Low                 |
| **Deprecated**                           | Plan to replace the package with a maintained alternative.                                             | Update documentation and inform the team.                                                               | Low                 |
| **Minified Code**                        | Review the original source if possible.                                                                | Ensure minified code doesn’t contain malicious elements.                                                | Low                 |
| **Floating Dependency**                  | Pin dependencies to specific versions.                                                                 | Monitor for updates and changes in dependencies.                                                        | Low                 |
| **Telemetry**                            | Review the data being tracked.                                                                         | Ensure compliance with privacy policies and regulations. Notify the team and stakeholders about the telemetry. | Low                 |
| **Obfuscated Code**                      | Perform a detailed code review.                                                                        | Replace the package if necessary. Maintain a log of such incidents for future reference.                | Technical           |
| **Dynamic Require / Uses Eval**          | Evaluate the necessity and safety of dynamic code execution.                                           | Refactor the code to remove or replace such practices where possible.                                   | Technical           |
| **Filesystem/Network/Shell Access**      | Assess the security impact of these accesses.                                                          | Ensure proper sandboxing and isolation. Monitor for any unusual activity involving these accesses.      | Technical           |
| **Install Scripts**                      | Audit install scripts for malicious behavior.                                                          | Replace packages with suspicious install scripts if necessary.                                          | Technical           |
| **High Entropy Strings**                 | Investigate the high entropy strings to determine if they are encrypted data, leaked secrets, or obfuscated code. | If necessary, refactor the code to remove or replace these strings.                                      | Technical           |
| **Network Access**                       | Review the module’s network access.                                                                    | Ensure that the network access is necessary and secure. Implement measures to monitor network activity. | Technical           |
| **Unmaintained**                         | Look for alternative packages.                                                                         | Schedule periodic reviews to ensure ongoing compatibility and security.                                 | Maintenance         |
| **Deprecated License**                   | Plan to replace the package with one that has a current license.                                       | Ensure all legal requirements are met and update the team on the status.                                 | Maintenance         |
| **Ambiguous License Classifier**         | Review the license classifier to understand the legal implications.                                    | Consult with the legal team if necessary and document the findings.                                      | Maintenance         |
| **Non-permissive License / Legal Notice**| Consult with the legal team to understand implications.                                                | Assess the risk of continuing to use the package. Document any legal concerns and inform stakeholders.  | Maintenance         |
| **Explicitly Unlicensed Item**           | Review the package for any licensing issues.                                                           | Consult with the legal team if necessary and document the findings.                                      | Legal and Licensing |
| **Copyleft License**                     | Understand the implications of using a copyleft license.                                               | Ensure compliance with the license terms and document any requirements.                                  | Legal and Licensing |
| **Non-OSI License**                      | Review the license to understand its implications.                                                     | Consult with the legal team if necessary and document the findings.                                      | Legal and Licensing |
